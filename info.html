Platform Guidelines

Authentic Followers Only

Accounts with fake followers, bots, or purchased engagement are strictly prohibited and will be removed.

Clear Communication

Sellers must provide accurate account information, including follower count, engagement rate, and any restrictions.

No Prohibited Content

Accounts promoting hate speech, illegal activities, or adult content are not allowed on the marketplace.

Fair Pricing

List accounts at reasonable prices based on market value. Price manipulation is monitored and penalized.

Prohibited Items

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does account transfer take?
Typically 1-3 business days after payment confirmation. The seller will provide credentials and guide you through the transfer process.
Is my payment secure?
Yes! We use encrypted payment processing and escrow protection. Funds are only released to the seller after buyer confirmation.
What if the account gets recovered?
We offer a 30-day warranty against account recovery. If the original owner reclaims the account, you get a full refund.
Can I sell multiple accounts?
Absolutely! Verified sellers can list unlimited accounts. Contact support for bulk listing options.
